held that an appeal is competent to the supreme court under section 476b of the code of criminal procedure from an order of a division bench of a high court directing the filing of a complaint for perjury.
also held that the simultaneous prosecution of civil and criminal proceedings regarding the same matter is likely to embarrass the accused and so ordinarily, and in the absence of special circumstances, the criminal proceedings should be given precedence and the civil proceedings should be stayed pending the termination of the criminal.
