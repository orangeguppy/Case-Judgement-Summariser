the supreme court unanimously dismisses the appeal by the complaints commissioner, holding that the commissioner had (i) no power to recommend the payment of a money sum against an individual who was not a public authority in an investigation under article 8 of the commissioner for complaints (northern ireland) order 1996 and (ii) no power to make a special report drawing the attention of the legislature to such a persons failure to comply with a recommendation. lord sumption gives the judgment, with which the other justices agree.
