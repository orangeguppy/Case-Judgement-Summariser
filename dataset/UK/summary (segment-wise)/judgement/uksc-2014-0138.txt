the supreme court unanimously dismisses mrs roberts appeal, holding that the safeguards attending the use of the s 60 power, in particular the requirements to give reasons both for the authorisation and for the stop and search, make it possible to judge whether the power has been exercised lawfully. both the power and the particular search of mrs roberts were in accordance with the law. lady hale and lord reed give the only substantive judgment with which the other justices agree.
